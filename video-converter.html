Convert your videos between different formats. Our tool supports MP4, AVI, MKV, and MOV formats. Upload your video and choose the output format you need.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why can't I convert between video formats in this tool?

A: Video format conversion requires heavy processing that browsers cannot handle securely. We provide direct download and links to specialized online video converters.

Q: Is there a file size limit?

A: Yes, we support videos up to 100MB for direct upload and download.

Q: Are these external conversion services free?

A: Yes, most offer free conversions for files up to certain sizes. They may have premium options for larger files.

Q: Is my video file secure?

A: Yes! Your video is processed locally and is never uploaded to our servers when using the direct download option.

Q: Can I use this on mobile devices?

A: Yes! The tool works on smartphones and tablets. However, for the best experience, we recommend using a desktop computer.
