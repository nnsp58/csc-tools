Text to Speech Converter

Convert your text into natural-sounding speech instantly. Our free online TTS tool uses your browser's built-in speech synthesis to generate audio from text. Perfect for accessibility, learning, and content creation.

How to Use

  1. Enter or paste the text you want to convert to speech
  2. Select a voice from the available options
  3. Adjust pitch if needed (optional)
  4. Click "Speak" button to hear the text

  • Multiple Voices: Choose from various voices available on your system
  • Pitch Control: Adjust pitch from low (0) to high (2)
  • Offline Support: Works without internet after initial load
  • Mobile Friendly: Works on smartphones and tablets
  • No Registration: Completely free to use

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is Text to Speech?

A: Text to Speech (TTS) is a technology that converts written text into spoken words. Our tool uses your browser's built-in speech synthesis capabilities to read text aloud.

Q: What voices are available?

A: The available voices depend on your operating system and browser. Common options include various accents and languages like English (US, UK), Hindi, Spanish, French, and more.

Q: Can I adjust the pitch?

A: Yes! Use the pitch slider to adjust from 0 (low) to 2 (high). Default is 1.0.

Q: Does Text to Speech work offline?

A: Yes! Our tool works completely offline after the page loads. It uses the browser's built-in speech synthesis engine.

Q: Can I use this on mobile devices?

A: Absolutely! The tool is fully responsive and works on smartphones and tablets. Voice availability may vary by device.
