Image Compressor Tool

Compress your images online for free. Reduce image file size without losing quality. Perfect for website optimization, email attachments, and saving storage space. All compression happens in your browser - your images are never uploaded to any server.

How to Use

  1. Upload your image by drag & drop or click to browse
  2. Select compression level (Low, Medium, or High)
  3. Click "Compress" to process your image
  4. Download the compressed image

Compression Levels

  • Low (High Quality) - Best quality, minimal compression
  • Medium - Balanced quality and size
  • High (Low Quality) - Maximum compression, smaller file

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What image formats are supported?

A: Our image compressor supports JPEG, PNG, and WebP formats. You can upload any of these formats for compression.

Q: How does compression level work?

A: We offer three compression levels: Low (80% quality), Medium (50% quality), and High (20% quality). Choose based on your needs.

Q: Will my image quality be affected?

A: Higher compression means smaller file size but lower quality. The Low option preserves most of the original quality.

Q: Can I use this on mobile devices?

A: Yes! The tool is fully responsive and works great on smartphones and tablets.

Q: Are my images secure?

A: Yes! All processing happens in your browser. No images are uploaded to our servers.
