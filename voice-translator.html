Voice Translator Tool

Translate your voice and speech in real-time. Speak in one language and get instant translation in another. Perfect for travelers, language learners, and international communication.

Browser Compatibility Notice: Voice translation requires Web Speech API which is only available in Google Chrome and Microsoft Edge.

Current browser:
For best experience, please use Chrome or Edge on desktop.

How to Use

  1. Select your source language (the language you'll speak)
  2. Select your target language (the language you want translation in)
  3. Click "Start Recording" and speak clearly into your microphone
  4. Wait for the translation to appear and hear it spoken aloud

Supported Languages

  • Indian Languages: Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, Marathi, Gujarati, Punjabi, Malayalam, Kannada, Odia, Nepali
  • World Languages: English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Arabic, Thai, Vietnamese, and more

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How does voice translation work?

A: Our Voice Translator uses speech recognition to convert your spoken words into text, then translates that text into your target language. The translated text can be displayed and/or spoken aloud using text-to-speech.

Q: What languages are supported for voice translation?

A: We support 50+ languages including Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Marathi, Gujarati, Punjabi, English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, and many more.

Q: Do I need to grant microphone permissions?

A: Yes, you'll need to allow microphone access when prompted. This is required for the speech recognition feature to work. We do not store any voice recordings.

Q: Is there a limit to how long I can speak?

A: For optimal performance, we recommend speaking in short phrases (up to 30 seconds). Longer recordings may take longer to process.

Q: Can I use voice translation offline?

A: No, voice translation requires an internet connection as it uses cloud-based speech recognition and translation services.
