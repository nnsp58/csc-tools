PDF Converter Tool

Convert your PDF documents to multiple formats instantly. Our free online tool lets you convert PDF to Word (DOCX), images (JPG, PNG), and SVG. All processing happens in your browser - your files are never uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy and security.

How to Use

  1. Upload your PDF file by dragging and dropping or clicking the upload area
  2. Select the output format you need (Word, JPG, PNG, or SVG)
  3. Click the convert button and wait for processing to complete
  4. Download your converted file instantly

Supported Formats

  • PDF to Word (DOCX) - Extract text and convert to editable Word document
  • PDF to JPG - Convert each page to high-quality JPEG image

  • PDF to SVG - Convert to scalable vector graphics

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What types of PDF files can be converted?

A: Our converter works best with text-based PDF files. Scanned PDFs or image-based PDFs may not convert accurately as they require OCR technology.

Q: Is there a file size limit?

A: Yes, we recommend PDF files up to 10MB for optimal performance. Larger files may take longer to process.

Q: Will the formatting be preserved in Word conversion?

A: We strive to maintain basic formatting including paragraphs and headings. Complex layouts, tables, and images may not transfer perfectly.

Q: Can I convert multi-page PDFs?

A: Yes! Multi-page PDFs are supported. For image formats (JPG/PNG), each page will be converted to a separate image and downloadable as a ZIP file.

Q: Is my PDF file stored on your servers?

A: No! All processing happens locally in your browser. Your files are never uploaded or stored on our servers.
