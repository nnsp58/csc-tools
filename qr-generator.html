QR Code Generator Tool

Create QR codes for URLs, text, contact information, and more instantly. Our free online QR code generator produces high-quality, scannable codes that work with all QR readers. Perfect for business cards, marketing materials, and personal use.

How to Use

  1. Enter the text or URL you want to encode
  2. Click "Generate QR Code" button
  3. Download or share your QR code

Supported Data Types

  • URLs - Website links, social media, etc.
  • Text - Plain text, notes, messages
  • Email - Email addresses with subject
  • Phone - Phone numbers
  • WiFi - WiFi network credentials

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What types of data can I encode?

A: You can encode URLs, plain text, email addresses, phone numbers, WiFi credentials, and more.

Q: Is there a character limit?

A: For best results, keep URLs under 2000 characters. QR codes have capacity limits based on data type.

Q: Can I customize the QR code?

A: Currently we generate standard black and white QR codes for maximum compatibility.

Q: Are these QR codes free for commercial use?

A: Yes! All generated QR codes are free for both personal and commercial use.

Q: Can I scan with any smartphone?

A: Yes! Our QR codes work with all QR code readers including iOS and Android camera apps.
